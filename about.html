My Story

I'm Lalith Kumar. They say,From the Deepest Pains comes the Highest Purpose. Several years ago,I was almost nothing,I was just an Innocent Featherhead Speech Impaired Fatso. I used to be Bullied,Beaten,Ridiculed,Oppressed and taken advantage of. And,One day somebody very dear to me,left me. That,hit me hard and I was deeply moved. Everyday the trauma kept on growing and somewhere there,I realised that I was almost in the mouth of a Psychological Depression. That day,I felt like somebody is tearing me apart from inside,pain was unendurable and,It was then I turned to God to help me and provide me a soft succor. That's when Divinity and Music entered into my life. And, My Healing Process started. That's where,I decided to Change Myself Holistically. It took me,months of hardwork and perseverance,continually learning,working and improving myself day in and day out,to have a complete Transformation. When I completely Healed Myself,I realised something.I realised,there are people out there who are hurt,in pain,having nobody to stand by them,I had God and Music in my rescue but,what about others? That's when I said to myself,I have to be there for people,I have to help people Heal and empower them to Live at their Best.And,That moment I found My Life's Purpose...
